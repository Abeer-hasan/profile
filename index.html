    Publications & Projects

  • Buckling of Pushrods Made of Superelastic Shape Memory Alloy (ICME 2019)  

    This work deals with the buckling of a typical pushrod of an overhead valve engine. The Pushrod made of Superelastic SMA was considered as a both-end-hinged column which is eccentrically loaded. Using mathematical modeling techniques, generalized mathematical formulas for bending moment and reduced modulus of SMA columns with circular cross-section was developed. Based on that sophisticated computer programs were written and using Numerical Analysis, Er – Δ, M – Δ and P – δ curves was traced for various parameters .

  • Project 1: Smart Wall Painter  

    The aim of this project is to design, develop and implement an automated wall painting machine. Stepper motors, Rollers, chains along with few other mechanical parts like bearing, sprocket, gears etc. were used for the operation of the machine and various electrical components i.e. capacitors, resistors, IC were used to design the main circuit which was programmed using Arduino.

  • Project 2: Heat Exchanger.  

    A small sized shell and tube heat exchanger was designed and constructed.
